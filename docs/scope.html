What We're Building

A web platform where Northeastern students sign in with their NU email and get a personal hub for their co-op journey. They can read anonymous co-op reviews from other Huskies, connect with alumni, track their applications, and find curated resources, all in one place.

What Students Will Be Able To Do
👤

Create a Profile

Sign up with a Northeastern email. Add their major, year, co-op cycle, and interests. This is the foundation everything else builds on.

💬

Read & Write Co-op Reviews

Browse anonymous reviews from students who've been on co-ops. Tagged by skills learned, tools used, and company culture. Students can submit their own review through a short survey. No names shown, no company secrets.

🤝

Connect with Alumni & Peers

Search a directory of students and alumni who've opted in. Filter by company, role, or industry. Reach out via email or LinkedIn. Only people who've given consent are listed.

📋

Track Applications

A personal tracker for all co-op applications. Update statuses (applied, interviewing, offered, rejected), attach resumes, and see a visual summary of the pipeline.

📚

Browse Resources

A curated page of links organized by industry: NU Library, LinkedIn, IBISWorld, interview prep, online courses, and professional clubs. Think of it as a bookmark page built for co-op students.

Why this split? The reviews, networking, and tracker are the features students will use day one. The assessments and recommendation engines are powerful but add significant build time. By launching with a focused first version, real students can start using the platform sooner, and their feedback shapes what gets built next.
Build Plan

Two phases. Get the platform live, then add polish.

Phase 1

Launch

Sign-up, profiles, co-op reviews (submit + browse), networking directory, application tracker, resources page, and email notifications. A complete, usable platform.

Phase 2

Enhance

Refinements based on user feedback, improved search and filtering, dashboard analytics, and any priority features from the "Later" list above.

Tools We're Using

You don't need to worry about the technical details. Here's what powers the platform, in plain terms.

⚛️ React: builds the pages you see Free
⚡ Supabase: stores data and handles sign-in Free
▲ Vercel: puts the site on the internet Free
🚂 Railway: runs behind-the-scenes logic ~$5/mo
📧 Resend: sends emails to students Free

Total estimated running cost: ~$5/month. Everything else is on a free tier that covers this scale comfortably.

Privacy & Safety

Reviews are completely anonymous. No names attached. The networking directory only shows students who've explicitly opted in. Only people with a Northeastern email can access the platform. No company-sensitive information is allowed in reviews.
